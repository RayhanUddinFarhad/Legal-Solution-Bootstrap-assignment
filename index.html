Business Law

Our firm provides comprehensive legal guidance for corporations, partnerships, and small businesses. We assist with formation, contract drafting, mergers and acquisitions, and other business-related matters.

Criminal Law

We have a team of experienced attorneys who specialize in criminal defense. We represent clients facing criminal charges, trials, and appeals. We are dedicated to protecting our clients' rights and fighting for the best possible outcome.

Child Support

Our attorneys provide legal assistance for child support agreements and modifications. We work with clients to ensure fair and appropriate child support arrangements that prioritize the best interests of the child.

Education Law

Our firm offers legal advice for students and educators on school-related issues. We help clients navigate complex education laws and regulations to ensure their rights are protected.

Divorce Law

We provide legal representation for divorce proceedings and related matters, such as property division, alimony, and child custody. Our attorneys are dedicated to helping clients navigate the difficult process of divorce in a compassionate and efficient manner.

Tax Law

Our firm offers legal advice and representation for tax-related issues, including audits and appeals. Our attorneys have extensive knowledge of tax laws and regulations and work with clients to minimize their tax liabilities and resolve disputes with the IRS.
